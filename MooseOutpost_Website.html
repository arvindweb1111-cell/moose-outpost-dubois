Our Story

HOME OF THE BIG MOOSE ON RAMSHORN

The Moose Outpost is Dubois, Wyoming's beloved burger stop — a cozy little place with friendly staff, fresh-made food, and flavours that keep travellers coming back every time they pass through on their way to Yellowstone.

Everything is made fresh to order. Our ¼lb ground chuck burgers are stacked with fresh fixings on homemade buns. Fries are hand-cut from whole potatoes. Shakes are hand-dipped in creative Wyoming flavours you won't find anywhere else — rhubarb, huckleberry, salted caramel and peanut butter banana.
